Frequently Asked Questions

Will products automatically update?

Yes. We keep track of what items are discontinued and what items are coming in to make sure your information is up to date.

Will this integrate with Ashley/Wondersign's kiosk?

Yes. We understand many stores already own a kiosk from Ashley/Wondersign and understand that purchasing another kiosk may not be feasible.

If you have already purchased access to the Homelegance Kiosk Application and need help installing it, check out our walkthrough.

Do I need to be connected to the internet?

Yes. Our application runs on a web browser and needs to be connected to the internet in order to function. We recommend having a relatively strong connection to ensure the best customer experience.

Will this work with my iPad?

Yes. The Homelegance Kiosk Application works with most devices. We recommend using anything that is the size of an Apple iPad or larger.
